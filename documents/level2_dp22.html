DP-22
Jones, Lead of development
Wells, Head of research team #2
20.12.2015

Description
DP-22 is a unique derium interpreter. It behaves almost like a traditional interpretor but with some changes. Its unique logic board is tuned to execute small SPELLs at a fast pace. DP-22 has been designed to match the shape of an ordinary pistol. This makes DP-22 very ideal for military purposes.

The SPELL magazine's maximum capacity is four (4) terabytes. This is enough to have clear variation between the spells.

DP-22 has to have a compatible energy source. Only derium capacitors of model DC-70 and above are allowed.


Differences from the predecessors
Energy input port is now parallel to the SPELL magazine. This makes weapon handling easier and more comfortable for long time use.
